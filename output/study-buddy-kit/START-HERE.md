# Hey! Meet your Study Buddy 👋

This is your own AI helper for school (and for whatever you're curious about). It's like having
a patient tutor who's awake 24/7 and never makes you feel dumb for asking.

**The big idea:** Study Buddy helps you *understand* things — it doesn't just do your work for
you. That's on purpose. The stuff you actually learn is the stuff that sticks. 💪

---

## What it can help you with

| When you want to… | Just say something like… |
|---|---|
| 🧩 **Understand something** | "Explain fractions like I'm in 6th grade." |
| ✏️ **Get un-stuck on homework** | "I'm stuck on this problem: ___. Where do I start?" |
| 📝 **Practice for a test** | "Quiz me on the water cycle, 5 questions." |
| 🃏 **Make study cards** | "Make flashcards from these notes: ___" |
| ✍️ **Improve your writing** | "Here's my essay intro — is it clear?" |
| 📖 **Understand a reading** | "I read this paragraph but I'm lost — what's it saying?" |
| 🔎 **Research a topic** | "Help me find good sources about volcanoes." |
| 🗺️ **Plan a big project** | "I have a science fair project due in 3 weeks — help me plan." |
| 🧠 **Get better at using AI** | "How do I ask a better question?" |

You don't have to memorize any of this — just talk to it normally and tell it what you need.

---

## A few things to know

- **It'll ask you questions.** That's the point — figuring stuff out *with* help makes you
  smarter than just being handed answers. Play along. 🙂
- **Stuck for real?** Say *"just show me,"* and it'll walk you through the whole thing and
  explain *why* — then let you try one yourself.
- **It won't write your essays for you.** It'll help you brainstorm, plan, and give you
  feedback — but the words have to be yours. (That's not it being mean — turning in AI writing
  as your own is cheating, and it means you didn't actually learn it.)
- **It might want to get to know you.** The first time, it may ask a few quick, fun questions —
  your grade, what you're into, how you like to learn — so it can help in a way that fits *you*.
  You can skip any question, and it never needs your last name, address, or school. Say "forget
  my profile" any time and it'll stop using it — and ask a grown-up to delete the saved copy too.

## 3 rules to keep you safe 🔒

1. **Never share private stuff** — your full name, address, school name, passwords, phone
   number, or photos. The AI doesn't need any of that to help you.
2. **AI can be wrong.** It sometimes makes things up! For anything important, double-check it
   with a real, trusted source. Ask it "how do I know that's true?"
3. **It's a program, not a person.** If something's really bothering you — you're feeling
   down, someone's being mean to you, anything scary — talk to a parent, teacher, or another
   grown-up you trust. Not a chatbot. **If you ever feel unsafe or like you might hurt yourself,
   tell a trusted adult right now — or in the US, call or text 988, or text HOME to 741741.
   Real people who want to help, any time.**
